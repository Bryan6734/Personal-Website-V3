About me

Hello there! My name is Bryan Sukidi and I'm a senior studying at Milton Academy. I grew up in Somerville, Massachusetts, but my family currently lives in Jakarta, Indonesia.

Over the past four years, I've become increasingly aware of the risks that AI poses to humanity. These risks have inspired me to pursue both computer science and public policy in my mission to ensure that AI benefits our world. In the future, I hope to conduct AI research on improving the interpretability, robustness, and fairness of AI sytems.

Aside from my academic interests, I love indoor rock climbing, playing guitar, and conversing about religion, music, literature, and more. Feel free to email me if you'd like to get in touch!
