About me

My name is Bryan Sukidi. I'm a first-year undergraduate at UNC Chapel Hill studying Computer Science and Statistics under a Morehead-Cain scholarship.

Over the past four years, I've become increasingly concerned with the risks that AI poses to humanity. I'm interested in working with large language models to help improve their robustness and interpretability.

Apart from that, I love indoor rock climbing, playing guitar, and listening to podcasts. Feel free to email me if you'd like to get in touch. I'm always looking for new opportunities!
